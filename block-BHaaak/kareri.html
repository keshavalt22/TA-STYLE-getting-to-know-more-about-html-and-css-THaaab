The camp at present is the only base camp and starting point for many famous hikes such as Kareri Lake trekking , Minkiani Pass, Baleni Pass, Lam Dal seven lakes and Druni Lake. It also offers the finest traverse trail of the Dhauladhar Himalaya starting from Snow Monk Camp covering Triund, Indrahar Pass and ending at Mcleodganj. We encourage all to come and adopt the culture of trekking and camping by providing all guidance and expertise like a one stop shop.

The camp set in the Khauli valley, is home to the lovely Khauli River and close to its Nyund River. The camp offers all necessary comforts of a base camp like accommodation in water proof tents with camp cots, pillows, blankets, electricity, charging points etc. Set in 5 platforms, the camp has 12 large tents which each can accommodate about 6-8 people. There are smaller trekking tents also available on request. The toilets are modern western with bathrooms having hot water provided by solar geysers. The overall experience is heightened by the delicious home cooked style food that is freshly cooked and served camping style.

The camp offers many engaging activities in adventure, culture, creativity and community service. The camp has partnered with the local village panchayat and there are ongoing projects throughout the year which makes it our USP offering curated CAS trips for IB schools. Being an approved TAP trained activity provider for the Duke of Edinburg awards program, we offer treks and expeditions for bronze, silver & gold level.

We encourage all our guests to be responsible trekkers and put as less load on the environment as possible by recycling as much as they can.

Kareri Lake Trek Itinerary

Day 1 - Dharamkot to Kareri Village (Height 2200m | Distance 8 KM)

- After arriving at Dharamkot and meeting the rest of the group, you will start with the trek to reach the Kareri Village.

- After meeting the people of Gaddi Tribe who are native to the Kareri region, you will spend the night in the tents or in one of the village houses.

Day 2 - From Kareri Village, trek to Kareri Lake (Height 3500m | Distance 13 KM)

- After breakfast this morning, you will be carrying packed lunch as you trek on your way to the Kareri Lake.

- You will be walking through forests of Oak, Rhododendron, and Pine.

- The Kareri Lake trek first passes through level grounds but then comes onto a steep ascent, in which you will be trekking alongside the Kareri Nullah Stream.

- From Kareri Village, you will go to Reoti Base Camp. From Reoti Base camp, trek to lake will be organised.

- Trek to lake from Reoti depends on weather conditions.

- You will be crossing over the rivulet a number of times using wooden bridges.

- Trek back to the Kareri village with the memories of the beautiful lake in your mind and in your camera.

- Overnight in Reoti Base camp with a campfire for your company.

Day 3 - Back to Mcleodganj

- After breakfast this morning, you will be taking the trek to Mcleodganj, where the Kareri Lake trekking experience will come to an end.
