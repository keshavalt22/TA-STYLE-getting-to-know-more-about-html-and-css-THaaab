Laka Glacier Trek is about 8 km from Bhagsu Nath and in all 13 km from Mcleodganj. Right from winters to June, there is snow and ice at the glacier. March to June is the best time for this trek. Post-June, glacier begins to decrease and by July/August, not much snow will be left.

This offbeat trek to the Laka Glacier offers some of the most amazing views of Dhauladhar mountain range and Kangra valley.

An interesting aspect of this trek is that it lies near the snowline, a mere trek of 13 km will take you to Laka Glacier. If you are looking for long-weekend trek, this is one of the most popular and beautiful trek that you can do in short duration. From July onwards the glacier starts to melt and during post monsoon period there is no snow at the glacier and you only see the moraines, whereas during pre-monsoon period (March to June) the glacier is full of snow and it is the best time to trek to Laka Glacier.

Your trek will commence at Bhagsu Nag, and from where the steep ascent towards Triund will begin. You will pass through Oak, Rhododendrons and deodar trees, till you reach a vast alpine meadow called Triund Top, from where you can spectacle the grandeur view of the Kangra Valley and Dhauladhar ranges.

Laka Glacier Trek Itinerary

Day 1 - Trek to Triund/Mana Camps (8 kms)

- Start your tour by arriving at the start point of the trek at 10.00 AM at Mcleodganj square.

- The trek to Triund begins from here which takes about 5 hours to complete. After reaching here, check into the campsite.

- Take a while to admire the Dhauladhar and Kangra Mountain ranges that greet you a welcome from Triund top.

- Enjoy a memorable dinner in the mountains. Overnight camping at Triund/Mana depending on permission.

Day 2 - Trek to Laka Glacier (6 kms)

- Start your morning with a wonderful breakfast.

- Trek on the Laka Got Glacier and Lahesh Caves trail, carrying packed lunch with you. Upon arrival at the glacier, enjoy the view. The trek is about 6 km.

- Have some fun in the glacier and snow around it. Take pictures and play with snowballs.

- Overnight camping near Laka Got after a wonderful dinner or Mana camps near Triund.

Day 3 - Trek back to Mcleodganj (14 kms)

- After breakfast this morning, you will start early to return directly to the Mcleodganj to end your trek.
